Mafu Mafu is an utaite artist known for his wide vocal range and soft, feminine voice. He began his career as a singer in December of 2010. He is currently 32 years old and continues to create music with voice effects while utilizing his voice range to create certain themes and characters.

He also collaborates with other utaites like Soraru, Urata, and Sakata.
